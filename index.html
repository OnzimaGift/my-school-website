Our Story

The school was started on 14th February 2013; it is a mixed boarding secondary school offering both Arts and Science subjects at Ordinary and Advanced levels. It is registered with the Ministry of Education and Sports and its registration Number is PSS/M295 and UNEB center Number is U3266, is a brain of experienced professional teachers who keen in promoting excellent school practices in the whole of Uganda as a whole.

Vision

To become a model school in the provision of quality education that meets the ever dynamic global environment.

Core Values

Mentor SS strive to achieve affordable, quality, and holistic education, which essentially is about working with different stake holders involving parents, teachers, students, and the community. To achieve this noble aim, Mentor SS cherishes its core values.These are:-

  • Impact and quality results
  • Trust and respect
  • Quality service
  • Integrity
